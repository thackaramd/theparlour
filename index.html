THE PARLOUR

A new literary magazine for all voices.

Introduction from the Editor

The Parlour is a magazine for writing that values presence as much as craft, where the work is alive, honest, and originating from the author’s lived experience.

By “presence”, I mean noticing the world, paying attention to thought and feeling, and genuinely sitting with yourself as you write. My hope is that contributors of any age feel encouraged to share their voices, take risks, and explore what’s real for them.
