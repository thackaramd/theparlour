THE PARLOUR

A new literary magazine for all voices.

Introduction from the Editor

The Parlour is for writing that has presence. We publish short fiction, poetry, essays, and even fragments. We welcome submissions that are honest, refined, and emotionally resonant. Remember, you're writing to the world and the world is listening. Each piece is an encounter.

SUBMISSIONS NOW OPEN

Submissions are now open for the first issue. The theme is 'Meeting', but it is optional. Email your work to submissions@theparlour.org.uk, including a brief bio and title. Deadline: 1st May 2026.

Max Thackara
Editor, The Parlour
